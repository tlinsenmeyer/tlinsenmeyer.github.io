## Welcome

I work at the intersection of building automation, HVAC systems, and data science. Most of my projects come from real operational problems—things that break, things that don't behave the way they should, or systems that could run smarter with better data and better logic.

This portfolio is a collection of the work I've done across simulation, analytics, machine learning, and applied statistics. Some projects are academic, some are practical, and some (like SIM-1) are long-term builds that keep evolving as I learn more.
